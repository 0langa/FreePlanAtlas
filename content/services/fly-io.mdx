Fly.io offers a generous always-free tier for deploying containerized applications globally, with simple CLI tools and a developer-friendly experience. The free tier is suitable for small projects, demos, and indie apps, but does not include free managed databases or large persistent storage. Overages are capped unless a payment method is added, reducing billing risk. Fly.io is ideal for developers seeking fast, global deployments with minimal setup.
