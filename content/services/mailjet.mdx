Mailjet offers a straightforward, always-free tier for sending up to 200 emails per day, making it suitable for small projects, transactional notifications, and basic marketing campaigns. The platform provides both API and SMTP integration, with basic analytics and support for essential email features. The free plan includes Mailjet branding and lacks advanced segmentation or dedicated IPs, so it's best for low-volume or early-stage use cases.
