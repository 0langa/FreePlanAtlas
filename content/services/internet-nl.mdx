Internet.nl provides a simple, free way to test your website, mail server, or internet connection for compliance with modern internet standards like IPv6, DNSSEC, and HTTPS. It is best for individuals or small teams needing quick, manual checks, but does not offer automation or API access for bulk or continuous testing.
