Font Squirrel is a popular resource for designers and developers seeking free, commercial-use fonts and a simple webfont generator. All fonts are hand-picked for quality and licensing, making it easy to find safe-to-use typefaces for both personal and commercial projects. The webfont generator allows you to convert fonts for web use, ensuring compatibility and performance. No account or payment is required, but users should always check individual font licenses for any specific restrictions.
