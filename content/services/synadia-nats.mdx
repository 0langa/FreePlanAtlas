Synadia NGS provides a managed NATS messaging platform with a generous free tier, suitable for prototyping, development, and small-scale production workloads. It excels at low-latency, real-time communication for distributed systems, but may not fit use cases requiring advanced message durability or enterprise-grade integrations.
