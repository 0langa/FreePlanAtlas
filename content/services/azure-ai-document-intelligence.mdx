Azure AI Document Intelligence provides AI-powered OCR and form recognition to extract structured data from documents, supporting automated workflows and digitization. The free tier offers 500 pages per month for key APIs, making it suitable for small to medium workloads. Overages are billed per page, and processing is cloud-based, so consider compliance and data residency requirements.
