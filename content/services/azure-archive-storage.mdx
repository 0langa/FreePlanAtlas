Azure Archive Storage is designed for organizations needing secure, durable, and cost-effective storage for data that is rarely accessed but must be retained for long periods. The free tier allows limited archival storage for up to 12 months, making it suitable for testing or small-scale archival needs. Retrieval times are slow and may incur additional costs, so it's best for compliance, backup, or regulatory scenarios where immediate access is not required.
