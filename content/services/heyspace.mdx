HeySpace offers a free plan for up to 5 users, combining chat and task management for small teams or projects. The platform is easy to set up and use, making it suitable for students, startups, and agencies needing basic collaboration tools. Storage and integration limits apply, so larger teams or those needing advanced features should consider alternatives.
