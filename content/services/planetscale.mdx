PlanetScale provides a serverless, MySQL-compatible cloud database with a generous free tier, ideal for prototypes, learning, and small production workloads. The platform supports branching workflows for schema changes, but the free plan lacks backups and private networking. It's well-suited for developers seeking managed SQL without operational overhead.
