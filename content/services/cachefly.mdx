CacheFly provides a generous always-free CDN tier suitable for static content delivery, with global edge locations and a 2 TB monthly bandwidth cap. It's ideal for indie developers, startups, and open source projects needing fast, reliable content distribution without upfront costs. Overages are billed automatically, so monitor usage if you expect high traffic.
