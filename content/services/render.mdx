Render offers a generous free tier for hosting static sites, web services, databases, and background workers, making it ideal for rapid prototyping and small projects. The platform automates deployments from Git, provides managed SSL, and includes basic monitoring. Free services may sleep when idle, and there are hard usage caps, but no risk of surprise overages. It's best suited for students, indie developers, and startups needing simple, reliable hosting without infrastructure management.
