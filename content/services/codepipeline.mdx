AWS CodePipeline is a managed CI/CD service that helps automate build, test, and deployment phases for applications and infrastructure on AWS. The free tier allows one active pipeline per month, making it suitable for small teams or projects starting with AWS automation. It integrates natively with other AWS developer tools and supports custom actions, but additional pipelines or integrations may incur charges. CodePipeline is best for AWS-centric workflows and teams looking for a managed, scalable CI/CD solution.
