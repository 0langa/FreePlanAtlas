Websitepulse.com provides a suite of free, web-based tools for manual website and server monitoring, diagnostics, and performance testing. No registration or payment is required, but the tools are limited to on-demand checks without automation or alerting. Suitable for quick troubleshooting and spot checks, but not for continuous or production-grade monitoring.
