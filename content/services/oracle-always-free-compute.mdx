Oracle Always Free Compute provides a reliable way to run small, persistent VMs at no cost, ideal for learning, prototyping, and open source projects. The free tier includes both ARM and AMD shapes, with generous RAM and storage for entry-level needs. While suitable for light production use, it is not intended for high-availability or large-scale deployments. Resources are capped and may be reclaimed if inactive, so monitor usage to avoid disruption.
