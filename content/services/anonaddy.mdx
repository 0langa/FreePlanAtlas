AnonAddy is an open-source email aliasing service that lets you create unlimited aliases to protect your real email address from spam and tracking. The free tier is suitable for individuals who want basic email privacy and are comfortable with bandwidth and recipient limits. It's easy to set up and ideal for students, indie users, or anyone needing simple, privacy-focused email forwarding.
