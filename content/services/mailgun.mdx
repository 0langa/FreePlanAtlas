Mailgun is a popular email delivery service offering APIs and SMTP relay for sending, receiving, and tracking transactional emails. The free tier allows new users to send up to 5,000 emails per month for the first three months, making it suitable for testing and early-stage projects. After the trial, charges apply automatically, so monitor usage and billing settings. Mailgun is best for developers and teams needing reliable email delivery, but not ideal for those seeking a permanent free solution.
