Rollgate is a managed reverse proxy and tunneling service that lets you securely expose local or private services to the public internet, ideal for development, testing, and demos. The free tier is suitable for basic use cases with limited bandwidth and no custom domains, making it a good fit for individuals and small teams needing quick, secure access to local services.
