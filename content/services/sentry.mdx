Sentry offers a generous free tier for error and performance monitoring, making it ideal for small projects, students, and open source maintainers. The platform provides real-time alerts, integrations with popular frameworks, and a simple setup process. However, teams needing more users, advanced analytics, or higher event volumes will need to upgrade to a paid plan.
