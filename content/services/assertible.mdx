Assertible offers a simple, always-free plan for API monitoring and automated testing, making it suitable for small projects and basic uptime checks. The free tier is limited to 2 services and 1000 tests per month, with basic integrations and email alerts. It is easy to set up and integrates with deployment workflows, but lacks advanced scripting and team features found in higher tiers.
