Font of web offers a simple, always-free CDN for open-source web fonts, making it easy to add typography to any website without bandwidth or usage limits. Ideal for students, indie developers, and open-source projects, it requires no signup or payment. However, it does not support custom or commercial fonts and does not provide enterprise-level guarantees.
