Mailtrap.io is a developer-focused tool for safely testing and debugging email sending without risk of spamming real users. Its free tier is ideal for individual developers or small teams needing to validate email flows in non-production environments, offering a single inbox and generous monthly limits. Mailtrap is easy to integrate with most frameworks and languages, making it a popular choice for email testing during development.
