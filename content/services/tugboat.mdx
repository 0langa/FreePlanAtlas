Tugboat lets teams and developers create on-demand preview environments for web applications, making it easy to review changes before deployment. The free tier is suitable for open source or small team projects with public repositories, offering limited environments and storage. Integration with popular version control systems streamlines the workflow, but private repo support and advanced features require a paid plan.
