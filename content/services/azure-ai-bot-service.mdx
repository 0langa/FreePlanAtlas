Azure AI Bot Service provides a managed platform for building, deploying, and scaling intelligent bots with integration to Azure and popular messaging channels. The free tier is suitable for prototyping and small-scale production use, with a generous monthly message allowance and easy integration with other Azure services. Advanced features and premium channels require upgrading to paid tiers.
