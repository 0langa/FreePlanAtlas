Zoom.us offers a robust free tier for video meetings, making it suitable for individuals and small teams needing reliable online collaboration. The 40-minute group meeting limit is a key constraint, but unlimited 1:1 meetings and up to 100 participants per session make it practical for most basic needs. Paid plans unlock longer meetings, advanced features, and more administrative controls.
