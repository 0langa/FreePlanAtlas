Visual Debug provides a simple way to collect visual bug reports and feedback directly on your website, making collaboration between developers, designers, and testers more efficient. The free tier is suitable for individuals or small teams needing basic bug tracking and feedback tools, but has limitations on projects and integrations. It's best for web-based projects in early-stage or light production environments.
