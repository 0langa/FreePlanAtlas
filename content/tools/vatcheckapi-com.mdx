Vatcheckapi.com offers a straightforward API for real-time validation of European VAT numbers, making it easy to automate compliance checks for EU businesses and applications. The always-free tier is suitable for low-volume use cases, such as startups or prototypes, but is not intended for high-throughput or mission-critical scenarios.
