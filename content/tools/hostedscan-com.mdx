Hostedscan.com offers a simple way to run automated vulnerability scans on your website or server, making it suitable for small projects or initial security checks. The free tier is limited to one scan per month for a single target, with no payment card required. This makes it a good fit for individuals or small teams needing basic, periodic security validation without advanced features or frequent scanning.
