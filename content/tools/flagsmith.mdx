Flagsmith offers a generous free tier for feature flag management, supporting unlimited flags and environments with a monthly request cap. It's ideal for teams and projects needing remote configuration or experimentation without complex setup. The platform is easy to integrate and supports open-source usage, but advanced enterprise features and self-hosted options require paid plans.
