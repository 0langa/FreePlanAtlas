Gemini API provides developers with access to Google's advanced generative AI models for building applications that require text, code, or multimodal understanding. The free tier is suitable for experimentation, prototyping, and low-volume production use, with clear monthly limits and no risk of surprise overages. Integration is straightforward via REST or SDK, and the service is ideal for teams looking to quickly add AI capabilities without managing infrastructure.
